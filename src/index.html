Stay safe during Covid 19.

Shopping Safety Tips

  • Wear a mask.
  • Shop smart and early.
  • Bring disinfectant wipes and sanitizer.
  • Keep your distance.
  • Checking out: use a credit card or cash app to pay. Give you cashier and bagger space.
  • Unpack and disinfect. Wash bags after use.
  • Order online if possible.
